Introduction

Our research interest lies on biomimetics,robotics and MEMS. 
Current research projects include: 
1.Biomimetic vision system: study biomimetic optical devices, micro-electromechanical systems, and image processing methods, such as bionic eye system, tunable fluidic lens based on elastic polymer membrane, accommodating intraocular lens, and visual attention model.
2.Robot intelligent perception and control: study the robotic motion control, SLAM and target recognition methods based on multi-sensor fusion (optical sensors, inertial sensors and force transducers), and design the biomimetic robot and perception system through analyzing and imitating the special structures and functions of natural organisms.

Recent research

1.Through imitating the optical structure and visual information processing of human eye, we have developed a biomimetic tunable lens system, fluidic lens and accommodating intraocular lens, and proposed a visual attention model based on double channel and multi-feature integration.


2.We have designed a robot grabbing and handling system based on visual information feedback, constructed a robot calligraphy copying system based on deep learning, and proposed a SLAM method based on Inertial / Magnetic sensor and monocular vision fusion.
